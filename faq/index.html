FAQ

Who can join AWS User Group Hong Kong?

Anyone interested in AWS, from beginners to experts, is welcome to join our community.

How do I join events?

Visit our Meetup page to RSVP for upcoming events.

Are the events free?

Yes, our events are free and open to everyone.

Can I speak at an event?

Absolutely! We welcome speakers from all backgrounds. You can propose a talk by filling out our Speaker Signup Form, or contact us via Meetup, Slack, or WhatsApp.

How can I stay updated?

Follow us on Meetup, YouTube, Slack, WhatsApp, and Facebook for the latest updates.
